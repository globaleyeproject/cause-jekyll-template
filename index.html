Our Mission

Our mission to establish high quality sustainable eye care throughout Haiti. Lack of adequate eye care is one of the most critical health problems facing rural Haiti, where there are just 3 optometrists. We have established a permanent eye care clinic, and are currently training local Haitians, to provide sustainable care.
